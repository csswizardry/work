A quick-turnaround, off-the-shelf, fixed price service I offer is remote code reviews. The review covers things like efficiency, architecture, sanity, architectural/scalability concerns and a lot, lot more.

How it works*:

  1. You give me access to your codebase.
  2. I spend a day assessing, evaluating and reviewing the CSS.
  3. I return a comprehensive review document the next day.
  4. Once you’ve digested the feedback, we have a Skype call to discuss the review and answer any questions you may have.
